About Us

We are a community-centered organization working to strengthen rural Tanzania through education, health, agriculture, and civic participation.

Who We Are

Think and Act for Tanzania

THINK AND ACT FOR TANZANIA (TATA) is a Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) registered under the Ministry of Health, Community Development, Gender, Elders, Women's and Children with registration number OONGO/R/0371.

The Organization was duly registered on August 06, 2019 and operates in Tanzania Mainland focusing on people with disabilities.

TATA is keenly focused on the needs of people living at the grassroots, where a large number of people (about 75%) reside in rural areas. NGOs like TATA serve as a supportive organ to accelerate the development of a country, supplementing government efforts to address shortfalls and fulfill the needs of Tanzanians.

Our motto, "Think big, start small, be part of change", guides our local development initiatives, as we believe the role to bring about change to the people and the nation is ours, together with the communities we serve.
