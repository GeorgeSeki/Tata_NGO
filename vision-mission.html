Vision & Mission

These principles guide every initiative we lead, every partnership we build, and every life we hope to strengthen.

Our Vision

To have communities who prosper socially, economically, and hygienically and that are able to reduce ignorance and diseases and enjoy a fulfilled and prolonged life.

Our Mission

Work with Tanzanian citizens in order to spearhead rural development for improving quality of life through education and empowerment. Our mission extends to aspects of health, education, agriculture, good governance, and road safety.

Our Goals

Strategic Objectives

We aim to achieve the following key objectives through our work:

01

Promote Good Governance and Awareness

Promote community awareness on good governance, road safety, and HIV/AIDS.

02

Facilitate Support for Vulnerable Groups

Facilitate Tanzanian citizens to support Albinos, deaf, cripples, PLWHIV, children, and women to acquire basic education, vocational training, and advocacy techniques.

03

Raise Awareness on Key Methodologies

Raising awareness to community on education, health, and agriculture methodologies and skills so as to fight against poverty.

04

Advocate for Social Services Provision

Advocate and support community in the provision of social services such as education, health, water, agriculture, and road safety services.

05

Staff Capacity Building

Training Organization staff team for career development in order to enable them to deliver quality service to Tanzanians.

06

Conduct Research and Apply Findings

Conduct researches and apply the research findings to address matters and bring positive change in development.

Our efforts also include collaborating with various groups (IGAs), Community Based Organizations (CBOs), NGOs (local and international), and other development partners to collectively fight against poverty across our five key thematic areas.

Our Work
